Mocha

Prep time : 5 mins

Yield : 1

Ingredients





Steps

  1. Use your preferred brewing method to make 8 ounces of coffee.
  2. Combine 1/4 cup chocolate shavings or cocoa with 1/2 cup of cream or milk..
  3. Heat chocolate and milk mixture slowly using a double broiler or microwave. If melting in a microwave, heat the mixture for 30-second intervals. Stir until creamy and fully combined.
  4. Add the warm chocolate and milk mixture to your coffee.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy.

Beef Tacos

Prep time : 15 mins

Yield : 10

Ingredients








Steps

  1. Cook beef in 10-inch skillet over medium heat 8 to 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until brown; drain.
  2. Stir salsa into beef. Heat to boiling, stirring constantly; reduce heat to medium-low.
  3. Cook 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Pour beef mixture into large serving bowl.
  4. Serve taco shells with beef mixture, lettuce, tomato and cheese.
